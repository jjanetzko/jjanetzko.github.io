Hello! I'm a soon-to-be Instructor in the Department of Molecular & Cellular Physiology at Stanford University in the lab of Brian Kobilka (who was also my postdoctoral mentor). I'm interested in using biochemical and biophysical methods to study how membrane proteins are trafficked and regulated in cells.

I did my PhD in Chemistry at Harvard, where I studied the essential human enzyme O-GlcNAc transferase (OGT). I was joint advised by Suzanne Walker, and Dan Kahne.

Before that, I studied synthetic organic and organometallic chemistry at the University of Toronto.

Outside the lab I can usually be found riding my bike all over beautiful California.
